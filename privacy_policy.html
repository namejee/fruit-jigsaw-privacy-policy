Fruit Jigsaw: Relaxing Puzzles Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 11, 2026 | Android package: com.jigsaw.relax.game

This policy explains how information may be collected, used, stored, and shared when you use Fruit Jigsaw: Relaxing Puzzles.

1. Information handled by the app

  • Local game data: level progress, coins, energy, entitlements, language, sound, music, vibration, and other settings are stored on your device.
  • Advertising data: this MVP release does not show ads. If advertising is enabled in a later release, Google AdMob and Google's User Messaging Platform may process IP address, device and app information, approximate location, diagnostics, ad interactions, app-set or similar identifiers, and consent choices. The app removes Android advertising ID and Privacy Sandbox ad-service permissions.
  • Purchase data: when Google Play Billing is enabled, Google Play and the app's verification service may process product IDs, purchase tokens, order and entitlement status, package name, app version, and verification data.
  • Support data: if you contact the developer, we may process the information you provide, such as your email address, screenshots, order reference, and issue description.

The app does not directly request access to contacts, SMS, microphone, photos, or precise location.

2. How information is used

  • Operate the game, save progress and settings, and restore eligible purchases.
  • Verify purchases, prevent duplicate fulfillment and fraud, and resolve support requests.
  • Collect and honor privacy choices required in the EEA, United Kingdom, Switzerland, and other regulated regions.
  • Comply with legal, security, app-store, and advertising-platform requirements.

3. Third-party services

The app may use Google Play Billing, Google Play services, and developer-operated purchase verification services. If advertising is enabled in a later release, the app may also use Google AdMob and Google User Messaging Platform. These providers process data under their own privacy policies and terms.

4. Storage and retention

Most gameplay data remains on your device and is normally removed when you clear app data or uninstall the app. Purchase verification, entitlement, fraud-prevention, support, and legally required records may be retained only as long as reasonably necessary for those purposes.

5. Your privacy choices

  • If advertising is enabled in a later release, where required, the app displays a Google consent message before requesting ads.
  • If Google requires a privacy-options entry, you can reopen Privacy options from the in-app Settings screen.
  • You can remove local data by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the app.
  • You may request access to or deletion of developer-held purchase verification or support data by emailing 184736819.jg@gmail.com. Include the app name and, when relevant, the Google Play order reference. Do not send a purchase token.

6. Children

The app is designed for a general audience, including children and families. This MVP release does not show ads. If ads are enabled in a later release, all ad requests will be treated as child-directed or of unknown age, personalized advertising and remarketing will be disabled, and ad content will be limited to Google's G rating. Children should use paid features only with permission and guidance from a parent or legal guardian.

7. Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect information. No transmission or storage method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

8. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated when the app, its service providers, or legal requirements change. The effective date above will be updated when revisions are published.

9. Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, email 184736819.jg@gmail.com and identify Fruit Jigsaw: Relaxing Puzzles in the subject line.
